SNAP course for specialists

Dive into the fascinating world of satellite image processing with our comprehensive course on SNAP (Sentinel Application Platform). Whether you're a beginner or looking to expand your skills, this course is designed to guide you through:

  • Getting Started with SNAP: Learn the fundamentals of the software and how to navigate its tools effectively.
  • Working with Optical Satellite Imagery: Explore techniques to process and analyze optical images from space.
  • Index Image Calculation: Master the computation of vegetation indices, water indices, and more.
  • Multitemporal Analysis: Discover how to compare satellite data across different time periods for deeper insights.
  • Clustering and Classification: Develop your skills in segmenting and classifying images using advanced algorithms.
  • Radar Image Processing: Unlock the potential of radar imagery with tailored preprocessing and analytical methods.

Join us on this journey to harness the power of satellite data for research, environmental monitoring, and beyond. Start your learning adventure today!
